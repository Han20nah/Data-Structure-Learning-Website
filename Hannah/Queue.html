What is a Queue?

A queue is a linear data structure that follows the First In, First Out (FIFO) principle. Elements are added from the back and removed from the front of the queue.

Features of Queue

  • First In, First Out (FIFO)
  • Enqueue and Dequeue operations
  • Used in scheduling and task management

Queue Example

queue.enqueue(5);
queue.enqueue(10);
queue.dequeue();
